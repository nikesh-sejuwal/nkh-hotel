About Hotel NKH

"Welcome to NKH, a place where comfort meets luxury. Our hotel is situated in the heart of Pokhara, offering easy access to all the city's top attractions.

We offer a wide range of rooms, from cozy standard rooms to spacious suites, all equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable and memorable stay. Our rooms feature comfortable beds, air-conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and complimentary Wi-Fi.

At NKH, we believe in making your stay as enjoyable as possible. That's why we have a range of facilities and services available, including a restaurant serving delicious cuisine, a bar serving refreshing drinks, a fitness center, and a business center.

Our friendly and attentive staff is always on hand to assist you with any needs, ensuring that your stay with us is nothing short of perfect.

Book your stay at NKH today and experience the ultimate in comfort and luxury."

-Nikesh
